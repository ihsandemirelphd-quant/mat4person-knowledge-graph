How this atlas was made

The MAT4Person pipeline reads archival biography sites, albums, and institutional histories, then asks the model one focused question per candidate pair: is there a documented relation — and where exactly does the text say so?

Only positive relations survive, each with its relation type, confidence grade, source path, page number, and exact quote. Nothing is drawn that cannot be pointed at.
